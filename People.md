# Team Members

## [Dr. K. Muthumeenakshi](https://www.ssn.edu.in/staff-members/dr-k-muthumeenakshi/)

**Dr.K.Muthumeenakshi,** is an Associate Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, SSNCE. She is the Principal investigator in the project titled "Automated Identification and Characterization of Underwater Images in Deep Ocean" and co-investigator in the project titled "Implementation of Magnetic Induction based Underground Sensor Network for Smart Irrigation".

## [Dr.  S. Sakthivel Murugan](https://www.ssn.edu.in/staff-members/dr-s-sakthivel-murugan/)

**Dr. S. Sakthivel Murugan,** currently serves as a Professor in the Department of ECE, NITTTR, Taramani, Chennai. He worked in the Department of ECE, SSN since June 2001 till September 2024\.  He has completed and ongoing research projects funded by MoES, DST-SSTP, TNSCST, NIOT, and SSN Trust work more than 200 lakhs.

## [Dr. G. Satheesh Kumar](https://www.ssn.edu.in/staff-members/satheesh-kumar/)

**Dr.G.Satheesh Kumar,** is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, SSNCE. He is associated with us as co-investigator in the project titled "Amphibious waterbody and beach cleaning BOT".

## [Dr. K. Murugesan](https://www.ssn.edu.in/staff-members/dr-k-murugesan/)

**Dr. K. Murugesan**, is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, SSNCE. He is associated with us as co-investigator  in the consortium project titled "Design and Development of Indigenous Research based Inspection Class Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) ".

## [Dr. N. Padmapriya](https://www.ssn.edu.in/staff-members/dr-n-padmapriya/)

**Dr. N. Padmapriya**, is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics, SSNCE. She is associated with us as co-investigator in the project titled "Off-shore excavation of heritage rich submerged sites of Poompuhar and Mahabalipuram by EMD using Deep Learning" .
